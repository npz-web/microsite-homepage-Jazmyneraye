Workouts

12x300m

300 meter intervals, jog/walk 100 meters for rest 

This workout is meant to help build up speed for distance runners. Whether you are training for a 5k, 10k, or something longer, these 300 will help you simulate that middle of the race speed.

Ladder Workout

1600m, 1200m, 800m, 400m, 800m, 1200m, 1600m, 60s rest in between 

Ladder workouts will help you focus on the turnover you need at the end of a race while also building up your strength. The quick decrease in distance is what keeps this hard workout engaging for all runners.

Mile Repeats

3x1600m

This classic workout can be done on the track or on the road, and is meant to help build up the endurance needed for long distance races. Its a good beginning of the season workout to get those conditioning miles in!
